Visualization of Climate Change

Introduction

For our mini-project, we selected data sets from NASA, the EPA, and Kaggle to identify different effects on climate change over the last 200 years. Our data includes global temperatures listed as regions, countries, and cities, CO2 emission levels, general climate change effects, and global temperature changes in realtion to a long-term average. We have created the below visualizations using tableau and have displayed them as interactive visualizations so that our audience can interact with the data and come to a more detailed conclusion about our planet's climate change.
